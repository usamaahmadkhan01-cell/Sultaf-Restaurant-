Pakistani & Middle Eastern Flavors

At Sultaf, we bring the best of Pakistani and Middle Eastern cuisine to Yogyakarta. From aromatic biryani and spicy chicken karahi to sweet kunafa and chai adeni every dish is made with authentic recipes and fresh ingredients.

Whether you're craving the warmth of Pakistani spices or the rich traditions of Middle Eastern cooking, Sultaf offers a menu that celebrates both worlds.
